# Vehicle Rental Manager - User Guide

Welcome to the Vehicle Rental Manager application. This guide will help you navigate the system to manage clients, vehicles, and reservations efficiently.

## 1. Getting Started

### Logging In
1.  Open the application in your web browser.
2.  If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the authentication page.
3.  Click the **Login** button to sign in using your Google account.
4.  **Note for New Users**: If this is your first time logging in, your account may require approval from an administrator before you can access the system.

## 2. Managing Clients

Navigate to the **Clients** page using the side menu. Here you can view, add, and manage your customer base.

### Adding a Client
1.  Click the **Create New Client** button at the top of the page.
2.  Fill in the required information:
    *   **Name**: Full name of the client.
    *   **Email**: Contact email address.
    *   **Phone**: Contact phone number.
    *   **Driver License**: Valid driver's license number.
    *   **Address**: Residential address.
3.  Ensure the **Active** checkbox is selected.
4.  Click **Save** to add the client.

### Searching and Filtering
*   **Search**: Use the search bar to find clients by name, email, phone number, or driver's license.
*   **Status**: Use the dropdown to filter between "Active" and "Inactive" clients.

### Editing or Deleting
*   Click **Edit** (yellow button) to modify a client's details.
*   Click **Delete** (red button) to remove a client from the system.

## 3. Managing Vehicles

Navigate to the **Vehicles** page to manage your fleet.

### Adding a Vehicle
1.  Click the **Create New Vehicle** button.
2.  Enter the vehicle specifications:
    *   **Make** & **Model** (e.g., Toyota Camry).
    *   **Color** and **Year**.
    *   **Price Per Day**: The daily rental rate.
    *   **License Plate**: The vehicle's registration number.
3.  Check the **Available** box if the vehicle is currently in service.
4.  Click **Save**.

### Searching and Filtering
*   **Search**: Find vehicles by make, model, or license plate.
*   **Status**: Filter by "Available" or "Unavailable".
*   **Year**: Filter vehicles by their manufacturing year.

## 4. Managing Reservations

Navigate to the **Reservations** page to handle rentals.

### Creating a New Reservation
The reservation process is divided into two steps to ensure vehicle availability.

**Step 1: Select Dates & Client**
1.  Click **Create New Reservation**.
2.  Select a **Client** from the dropdown list.
3.  Choose the **Start Date** and **End Date** for the rental.
4.  Click **Next: Choose Vehicle**.

**Step 2: Choose Vehicle**
1.  The system will display a list of vehicles available for the selected dates.
2.  Click **Select** on the desired vehicle. The system will automatically calculate the **Total Price** based on the duration and daily rate.
3.  (Optional) Add any **Notes** regarding the reservation.
4.  Click **Create Reservation** to confirm.

### Managing Reservation Status
*   **Active**: The reservation is currently valid or in progress.
*   **Complete**: Click the **Complete** button when a vehicle is returned to finalize the rental.
*   **Cancel**: Click **Cancel** if the client wishes to cancel the reservation.
*   **Edit**: Modify dates or vehicle for an active reservation.
*   **Delete**: Permanently remove a reservation record.
